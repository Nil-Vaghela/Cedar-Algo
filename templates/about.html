About Us

Cedar AlgoTrading is dedicated to democratizing algorithmic trading, making advanced financial tools accessible to everyone. Founded by a group of passionate software developers and finance professionals, we strive to empower traders and investors by providing cutting-edge, easy-to-use trading software tailored for educational purposes.

Our mission is to make algorithmic trading more transparent and accessible, reducing barriers for individual traders and educators. Whether you're a beginner looking to understand the basics of trading algorithms or a seasoned educator in finance, Cedar AlgoTrading provides the tools and resources you need to succeed.
