API Key Setup Guide

1. Username (Client ID)

Your username is your client ID. Go to your profile to find your Client ID.

2. PIN

Your 4-digit PIN which you use to log into your Angle One account.

3. API Key

Visit https://smartapi.angelbroking.com, log in with your Angle One account, create an app, select "Trading API", name your app "Cedar", and set the redirect URL to https://www.cedaralgo.in. Click create app and copy the API key provided.

Click enable TOTP from the top, sign in with your Angle One account and copy the key as shown in the screenshot.
